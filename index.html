About me

Introductions are in order

Kevin LeBlanc

Web Designer & Web Developer from Toronto

Which do you like more, art or science? Personally, I have never been able to answer that question. A strong interest in both is what led me to complete my undergrad in physics and philosophy at the University of Guelph. It was there that I developed a knack for straddling both realms of art and science, which came into play later when I worked as a freelance photographer and videographer.

I have always loved bringing life to things that didn’t exist before, and that is what attracted me to the world of web development. First learning front-end, and then enrolling in Bitmaker’s full-stack development bootcamp, I fell in love with the possibilities for creation. From design to code I am passionate about creating web experiences that are beautiful in both their form and function, and am now working as a freelance full-stack developer with an appreciation for UX design.
